## Overview

The Rundisliga Cup is an annual fantasy football cup competition running alongside the main FPL H2H league. It features a Swiss-style group stage (like the UEFA Champions League) followed by knockout rounds.

### Format
- **20 managers** compete
- **Group Stage**: 10 rounds - each team plays 10 different opponents
- **Knockout Stage**: Top 8 qualify for Quarter-Finals, Semi-Finals, and Final
- **Final**: Last gameweek of the FPL season

### Scoring
- Win: 3 points
- Draw: 1 point
- Loss: 0 points
